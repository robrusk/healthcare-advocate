# Appeal Template: Medicare Advantage (Part C) — Medical Necessity Denial

## Legal Framework
- Governing law: 42 C.F.R. Part 422, Subpart M
- Appeal path: Reconsideration → Independent Review Entity (IRE) → ALJ → Medicare Appeals Council → Federal Court
- Do NOT cite ERISA — Medicare Advantage plans are governed by 42 C.F.R. Part 422, not ERISA
