# Healthcare Advocate
### Fighting Insurance Denials. Free. Private. On Your Side.
**healthcareadvocate.org**

---

## The Problem

Every day in America, families receive insurance denial letters written in dense legal language, citing codes they've never heard of, with deadlines as short as 72 hours. The person reading that letter isn't the patient — it's an exhausted adult child, a frightened spouse, someone who doesn't know where to start.

What most families don't know:
- **73 million** insurance claims are denied every year
- **Less than 1%** are ever appealed
- **50–80%** of appeals succeed when families do fight back

The denials are generated by AI. The families have nothing.

---

## The Solution

Healthcare Advocate is a free web app that puts a knowledgeable advocate in every family member's pocket — available at 2am, on any phone, at no cost.

**How it works:**
1. Photograph or upload the denial letter
2. AI reads it and explains it in plain English — written for a 75-year-old with no insurance background
3. The family reviews and confirms the key facts
4. Three ready-to-send letters are generated: **insurance appeal**, **hospital letter**, and **doctor support letter**
5. Legal citations come from vetted official sources — CMS handbooks, ERISA regulations, Medicare rules — not AI guesswork

**What makes it different:**
- **Legally grounded** — a plan-type routing table prevents the most dangerous error in insurance appeals: citing the wrong law (e.g., ERISA on a Medicare denial gets the appeal dismissed)
- **Verified sources** — every letter is grounded in curated official documents, not AI training data
- **Fully private** — no medical data leaves the user's device to generate the document library
- **Free forever** — core functionality will never be paywalled

## Technology

Built on React, deployed as a Progressive Web App — installs on any iPhone or Android from the browser, no App Store required. AI processing runs through a secure backend proxy; no API keys or medical data are ever exposed in the browser. The legal framework library is curated by hand and updated annually — the AI adapts templates, it does not invent citations.

---

## Governance

Healthcare Advocate is being structured as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. The founding bylaws will include a mission-lock clause requiring unanimous board approval to change three core principles:
- Patients first, always
- Free forever for the family
- No advertising, no data selling, no conflicts of interest
